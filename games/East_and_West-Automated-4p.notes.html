East & West is an Axis & Allies style wargame published by Imp Games in 2000. It is about a hypothetical World War 3 occurring in 1948.

The game uses standard Axis & Allies rules for the most part. Not all rules for East & West are supported in this mod. You should check the unit help for the information about individual units.

Armor In E&W, land units may move out of newly captured areas. This is not supported.

Battleships Battleships are 2 hit but there is no provision for repair.

Unit Placement E&W allows infantry to be built without a factory. This hands Russia too big an advantage and is not supported.

Victory Conditions This mod allows the West to win by taking Moscow and the East to win by taking France, Britain or the Eastern US. There is also an economic victory at 90 for the East. This simplifies the rules from E&W.

Atomic Bombs These rules are considerably simplified to be handled by the TripleA engine. They inflict 5 hits (10 for fusion). Players should not combine with a conventional attack but TripleA does not prevent that. TripleA prevents any units with the bomb from firing until the unit is removed at the end of the attack. Bombs do not need to be carried by a bomber, but they are treated as one use in bombers in their own right. Players should not attack without a landing area but TripleA does not enforce that limitation. There are no nuclear complications. The AI will not generally build bombs.

Spies Spies are not used. Their actions are reflected in random events.

TechnologyThe complex technology rules of E&W are not used. Instead all technologies have been configured as new unit types. Each major power has a turn# in 20 chance of receiving the tech (at the beginning of their turn) as well as a free prototype unit. Existing units are unaffected by the new technologies. The prices of more advanced units have been slightly increased. The effects of some techs have been modified to conform with TripleA.

Minor Neutrals Minor neutrals are controlled by a power called "Non_Aligned". Each major power has a turn# in 80 chance each turn of gaining control. The units do not change control when the territory does. The East alliance can attack neutrals, the West alliance can't.
Neutral Alliances are handled differently than E&W. Each territory is treated separately like with minor neutrals. Chance of gaining control is
OAS: Turn#/15 of joining the United States, Turn#/25 of joining the Soviet Union
Arab League: Turn#/20 of joining Western Europe, Turn#/20 of joining the Soviet Union
China: Turn#/30 of joining Britain, Turn#/10 of joining the Soviet Union

Russia starts with a default bid of 20
