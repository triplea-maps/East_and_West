East & West is an Axis & Allies style wargame published by Imp Games in 2000. It is about a hypothetical World War 3 occurring in 1948.

This mod uses the V3 (Anniversary Edition) rules instead of the rules from East & West for the most part.

Victory Conditions This mod allows the West to win by taking Moscow and the East to win by taking France, Britain or the Eastern US. There is also an economic victory at 100 for the East. This simplifies the rules from E&W.

Alliances The Eastern alliance is Soviet_Union, China and Arab_League. The Western alliance is United_States, Britain & Western_Europe

Minor Neutrals Minor neutrals are controlled by a power called "Non_Aligned".The East alliance can attack neutrals, the West alliance can't.
